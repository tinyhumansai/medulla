# Open Benchmarks, Open SDKs

The model is gated. The tooling around it is not.

## Open Benchmarks

Every fixture behind our published numbers ships in the open: the heavy fan-out corpora, the noise and injection stress tests, the steering and dependency-chain scenarios, the Euler-100 set, and the harness that runs them with strict offline scoring against ground truth. Run them against Medulla, run them against your current stack, run them against anything. We publish our numbers because you can check them.

## Open SDKs

The SDKs are open source as well: wiring in your models and tools, streaming fleet input, tracing every cycle end to end. You can see exactly how your harnesses will talk to the orchestrator before you ever request access.

Medulla is model-agnostic by design: route deep reasoning to your most capable model and routine work to cheaper ones, on any provider. Every published result was produced with off-the-shelf models you can rent today.
